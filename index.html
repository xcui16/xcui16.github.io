Exploring total value of U.S. agricultural trade (imports and exports) in 2024, monthly

According to the USDA Economic Research Service from the U.S. Department of Commerce and Bureau of the Census, U.S. agricultural imports were generally higher than exports throughout the year 2024. The total value of imports was unstable and peaked in October. In comparison, the total value of exports presented a declining trend from February to June. Then, it recovered significantly in October. This demonstrates that the U.S. experienced a trade deficit in agricultural products last year. Also, these trends suggest potential seasonal influences on agricultural trades.
